Introduction

Hello, this webtool is made for students by students. It was created as one component of a blended learning expierence. Every test, game or basically anything to click, can be reapeated as often as you like. Also nothing is restricted, which means if you want to jump to the end of an unit just click the 'Next'-button. But there is no possibility to save, so if you refresh the page your inputs are lost. Although we tested the tool quite extensively there can be always some error. To fix it you have to reload the page.


We highly recommend that you keep a journal with notes, like any topic related question that might arise (Don't forget to ask your professor the next time). This will help you to keep track of the things you learned as well as your progress.
