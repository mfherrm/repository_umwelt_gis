Introduction

This webtool was made for students by students. It was created as a component of a blended learning experience. Every test and game, can be reapeated as often as you like. There are no restrictions as well, which means that jumping to the end of a unit is possible by clicking the buttons labeled 'Next'. Any inputs will be lost when the page is refreshed. Although the tool was tested extensively there is always a possibility of bugs. In those cases, reloading the page might help. For the best experience using fullscreen is recommended.


We highly recommend that you keep a journal where you write down your impressions, any topic related questions as well as general notes. This way questions will be resolved and exam prep doesn't take quite as long. It will also help you to keep track of the things you learned as well as your progress.

Webtool Overview

Unit 1 – Mental Maps & Statistics Basics

This unit is split into two parts. The first part, Unit 1a will test your 'mental map' of the world. The second part, Unit 1b is an introduction to statistical values.


Unit 2 – Population Pyramids

This unit will make you acquainted with a special kind of histogram, the population pyramid.


Unit 3 – Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

This unit is split into two parts. The first part, Unit 3a serves as an introduction to the Sustainable Development Goal 1.2.1. The second part will focus on Sustainable Development Goal 4.2.2.


Unit 4 – Correlation

This unit is an introduction to the concepts correlation and regression.
