Unit 4 - Multiple Choice Test

This multiple choice test will quiz you on your knowledge of correlation and regression.

This test contains nine questions. Answer each one according to your knowledge and what you learned in the last unit. The test can be repeated as many times as you want. There is no required score to pass this test.

How it works

After clicking on 'Start quiz' you will have to pick one of up to three answers. At the end of the quiz you will be able to see your results and compare them with the right answers, as well as restart the test.
