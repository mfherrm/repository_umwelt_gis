Absolute & relative Solution

Absolute values describe the total number of something e.g. a count, the total population/total area of Germany or Kenyas county 'Nairobi'. Whereas relative values describe a number in relation to another e.g. a proportion like South Africa is 2.1 times larger than Kenya or a percentage. Relative numbers are easier to compare than absolute numbers. Always keep in mind how a relative is calculated.
